About:
This webmap is ment to show earthquakes in California from 1906-2016 and is ment to help show people how many earthquakes actually happen in the area. We want to provide an interactive map showing statistics of earthquakes in this region over time.
Services:
This app shows points of origin of earthquakes dots where they all happend and has an option to show magnitude and a filter for dates if you wanted a certian year
Additional Details:
Users can change the date by clicking and moving the sider around. The checkboxes on the right side can be used to filter the data by magnitude of the earthquakes. You can also click on the points in order to see information about that particular earthquake.
